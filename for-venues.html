How we make money:

A small ₱15 convenience fee is added to each online booking — charged to the player, not to you.

You keep every peso of your court rates.

✅ No setup fee — ever ✅ No monthly subscription ✅ No commission on your revenue ✅ No per-booking fee for you ✅ No long-term contract ✅ Cancel anytime

The convenience fee model: When a player books online, ₱15 is added to their total. You collect the full amount (your rate + ₱15) from the player. At the end of each week, we send you a weekly invoice for the total convenience fees collected from your online bookings. You pay us the convenience fees, and you keep 100% of your court rates. It's that simple.

💡 You can view your weekly invoice and mark it as paid right from your dashboard. We'll handle the rest.
