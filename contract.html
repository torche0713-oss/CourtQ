This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Apex Mind Agency ("Provider", "we", "us") and the venue owner identified below ("Client", "you"). This Agreement governs the use of the CourtQ Pro platform for venue management services.

1. Services Provided

The Provider grants the Client access to the CourtQ Pro platform (the "Service"), a web-based venue management system that includes:

  • Court booking and scheduling
  • Queue management with Open Play modes (4 on/4 off and Winners Stay & Split)
  • Public booking portal with GCash/Maya payment integration
  • Admin dashboard with real-time court management
  • Financial reporting with revenue tracking, cost management, and CSV export
  • Staff and co-owner management
  • AI-powered insights (Suggest, Briefing, Insights, Pricing)
  • Booking confirmations and status management
  • Leaderboard and player history
  • Session management with skill-based pairing
The Service is provided as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution. No hardware, servers, or on-premise software is provided under this Agreement.

2. Pricing — Free Forever

CourtQ Pro is 100% free for venue owners. There is no setup fee, no monthly subscription fee, no commission, and no minimum term. The Client pays nothing to use the platform.

How it works: A small convenience fee of ₱10 is added to each online booking — charged to the player, not the venue. The Client keeps 100% of their court rates. The convenience fee is collected by the Provider at the point of sale (online checkout) as the platform's sole revenue source.

What's included at no cost: Court booking and scheduling, Open Play engine, public booking portal with GCash/Maya payment integration, admin dashboard, financial reporting with CSV export, staff and co-owner management, AI-powered insights, leaderboards, live TV display, mobile scoring, and tournament engine — all premium features from Day 1. No tier upgrades, no upsells, no feature gates.

Walk-in bookings and POS transactions made directly by the venue admin are not subject to the convenience fee — it applies only to online bookings through the public portal.

This Agreement has no fixed term. The Client may begin using the platform immediately upon registration and may cease at any time. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice. There are no early termination fees, cancellation penalties, or minimum commitment periods.

4. Payment Terms

  • The ₱10 convenience fee is collected by the Provider directly from the player during online checkout. The Provider retains this fee as compensation for the platform.
