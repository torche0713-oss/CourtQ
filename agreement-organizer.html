Dear Organizer / Club Partner,

Thank you for choosing CourtQ Pro to power your Open Play sessions and community events. This Letter of Agreement ("Letter") summarizes the terms under which we will provide the CourtQ Pro platform to you as an organizer or club account.

1. The Service

We will provide you access to CourtQ Pro, a web-based platform that includes an Open Play engine with multiple rotation modes (4 on/4 off, Winners Stay & Split, Round Robin), scheduled session management, player queue, public registration portal, convenience fee invoicing, and a personal organizer dashboard. You may host sessions at any venue — whether registered on CourtQ Pro or not — by specifying the venue name during session setup. As long as courts are booked through CourtQ Pro Open Play, the platform fully supports it.

2. Pricing — Free Forever

CourtQ Pro is 100% free for organizers and clubs. No setup fee. No monthly fee. No subscription.

How it works: A convenience fee of ₱5 per player is charged per Open Play session. An invoice is generated automatically per session once the session reaches its designated player limit. You pay each invoice via the platform (GCash or Maya) and provide a transaction reference number for verification. You keep any entry fees, player contributions, or other revenue you collect.

3. Account Verification

To activate your organizer account, you must upload a valid government-issued photo ID through the registration form. This ID is uploaded securely via Cloudinary and is used solely for identity verification. Your account will remain inactive until verification is complete and approved.

4. Term

Free Forever: No fixed term. No monthly bill. No commitment. Either party may terminate at any time with notice.

5. Payment

There are no monthly fees. Each time an Open Play session you created reaches its target player count, a per-session invoice for ₱5 per confirmed player is generated. You pay each invoice through the platform by GCash or Maya, providing the transaction reference number. Unpaid invoices will be flagged in your dashboard.

6. Your Responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate organization information, honor scheduled Open Play sessions, keep your login credentials secure, and maintain a safe environment for players at your events. You are responsible for verifying your identity with a valid government-issued ID during registration.

7. Our Responsibilities

We commit to keeping the platform running (target 99.9% uptime), protecting your data, providing support via email, and continually improving the service. We process identification document uploads via Cloudinary for secure storage and verification.

8. Data

Your data is yours. We will never share or sell it. Data is stored securely on Google Cloud (Firebase). Government IDs are stored via Cloudinary and accessed only for verification purposes.

9. Limitation of Liability

We are not liable for disputes between organizers and players, third-party payment issues (GCash, Maya), or events beyond our control. The platform is provided "as is" with a target of 99.9% uptime.
