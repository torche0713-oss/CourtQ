This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Apex Mind Agency ("Provider", "we", "us") and the organizer or club identified below ("Client", "you"). This Agreement governs the use of the CourtQ Pro platform for organizing Open Play sessions and community pickleball events.

1. Services Provided

The Provider grants the Client access to the CourtQ Pro platform (the "Service"), an organizer-focused web application that includes:

  • Open Play engine with multiple rotation modes — 4 on/4 off, Winners Stay & Split, and Round Robin
  • Scheduled session management with date, time, court count, and player limit configuration
  • Player queue with skill-based pairing and automatic team assignment
  • Public registration portal for players to join Open Play sessions online
  • Per-session convenience fee invoicing (₱5 per player)
  • Organizer dashboard with real-time session control
  • Player confirmations and payment tracking
  • Session history and player leaderboard
  • Looping mode for continuous play rotation
  • Virtual court assignment (no physical venue required)
The Service is provided as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution. No hardware, courts, or on-premise software is provided under this Agreement. The Client may host sessions at any venue — whether registered on CourtQ Pro or not — by specifying the venue name during session setup. As long as courts are booked through CourtQ Pro Open Play, the platform fully supports it.

2. Pricing — Free Forever

CourtQ Pro is 100% free for organizers and clubs. There is no setup fee, no monthly subscription fee, no commission, and no minimum term. The Client pays nothing to use the platform.

How it works: A convenience fee of ₱5 per player is charged per Open Play session. An invoice is generated automatically per session once the session reaches its designated player limit. The Client pays each invoice through the platform (GCash or Maya), providing a transaction reference number for verification. The Client keeps any entry fees, player contributions, or other revenue they collect from players.

What's included at no cost: Open Play engine with 4 rotation modes, scheduled sessions, player queue, public registration portal, per-session invoicing, organizer dashboard, confirmations, leaderboard, looping mode, and virtual courts — all features from Day 1. No tier upgrades, no upsells, no feature gates.

3. Account Verification

Prior to activation, the Client must upload a valid government-issued photo ID (e.g. passport, driver's license, national ID) through the registration form. The ID is uploaded via Cloudinary (encrypted HTTPS) and stored securely. It is accessed solely for identity verification by the Provider. The Client's account will remain inactive until the ID has been reviewed and approved.

The Client represents that the identification document provided is genuine, unaltered, and belongs to the account registrant.

4. Term

This Agreement has no fixed term. The Client may begin using the platform immediately upon registration and account activation. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice. There are no early termination fees, cancellation penalties, or minimum commitment periods.

5. Payment Terms

  • The Client owes no monthly fees to the Provider. The platform is free to use.
  • A convenience fee of ₱5 per confirmed player is invoiced per Open Play session once the session reaches its designated player limit.
  • Invoices are payable through the platform via GCash or Maya. The Client must provide a valid transaction reference number for each payment.
  • The convenience fee is non-refundable once a session has been completed, except in cases of Provider error.
  • Unpaid invoices will be flagged in the Client's dashboard. Persistent non-payment may result in account suspension.
