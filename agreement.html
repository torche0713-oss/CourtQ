Dear Venue Partner,

Thank you for choosing CourtQ Pro to power your venue's booking and management needs. This Letter of Agreement ("Letter") summarizes the terms under which we will provide the CourtQ Pro platform to you.

1. The Service

We will provide you access to CourtQ Pro, a web-based venue management platform that includes court booking, queue management, public booking portal (with GCash/Maya payment integration), admin dashboard, financial reporting, staff management, venue logo upload, and AI-powered insights. You keep 100% of all booking revenue — there are no platform fees for venue owners.

As long as courts are booked through CourtQ Pro Open Play, the platform fully supports it.

2. Pricing — Free Forever

CourtQ Pro is 100% free for venue owners. No setup fee. No monthly fee. No commission. You keep 100% of your court rates.

How we make money: A small convenience fee (₱5/hr) is added to each online booking — charged to the player, not to you. You collect the full amount (your rate + ₱5/hr) from the player. We send you a weekly invoice for the total convenience fees collected. You pay us the convenience fees, and you keep 100% of your court rates.

3. Term

Free Forever: No fixed term. No monthly bill. No commitment. Either party may terminate at any time with notice.

4. Payment

There are no monthly fees. You collect your court rate plus the ₱5/hr convenience fee from each player at checkout. At the end of each week, we send you an invoice for the total convenience fees collected from your online bookings. You pay us the convenience fees, and you keep 100% of your court rates. Weekly invoicing ensures accurate tracking.

5. Your Responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate venue information, honor confirmed bookings, keep your login credentials secure, and maintain a safe environment for players. You set your own court pricing. Venue logos uploaded through the platform are processed via Cloudinary.

6. Our Responsibilities

We commit to keeping the platform running (target 99.9% uptime), protecting your data, providing support via email, and continually improving the service.

7. Data

Your data is yours. We will never share or sell it. Data is stored securely on Google Cloud (Firebase). Venue logos are stored via Cloudinary image hosting.

8. Limitation of Liability

We are not liable for disputes between you and your players, third-party payment issues (GCash, Maya), or events beyond our control. The platform is provided "as is" with a target of 99.9% uptime.

By signing below, you agree to the terms outlined in this Letter of Agreement. This is a legally binding document.
